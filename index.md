# The Hard Win — Policies

Public policy pages for **The Hard Win**, an inspirational history and culture content system
for Instagram. These pages are provided for transparency and for Meta app review.

- [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY) — what public Instagram data is processed, why, and how it's protected.
- [Terms of Service](TERMS_OF_SERVICE) — what the account is, and how comments are moderated.
- [Data Deletion Instructions](DATA_DELETION) — what data may be stored and how to request its deletion.

*These pages contain no secrets, tokens, or private credentials.*
