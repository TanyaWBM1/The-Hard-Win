person becomes a drafted card.

## Where finished work goes
- **Verified, drafted card records** (the audit trail with the 2+ sources) live in
  [`skills/the-hard-win/records/`](../skills/the-hard-win/records/), not here.
- This `research/` folder is for **pre-verification** notes — leads, candidate names, raw source
  links — before they pass the two-source rule.

## Naming
`person-name-topic.md`, e.g. `ida-b-wells-black-journalism.md`.

## The bar before a note becomes a card
- A real person who did the **hard version** of something.
- A **receipt**: at least two independent, reputable sources for the central fact.
- See [`CONTENT_RULES.md`](../CONTENT_RULES.md). A great story with no receipt is not a card.
