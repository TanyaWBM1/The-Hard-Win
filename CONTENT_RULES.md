# Content Rules — The Hard Win

The editorial standards every Hard Win post must meet. These are the **brand** rules
(stable). For **platform** rules that change often (hashtag cap, placement, originality
penalties), the authoritative source is
[`skills/the-hard-win/INSTAGRAM_PLAYBOOK.md`](skills/the-hard-win/INSTAGRAM_PLAYBOOK.md).

> Stub / living document. Expand as the system grows.

---

## 1. Truth first (the receipt)
- Every card features a **real person who did the hard version of something**, paired with a
  **receipt** — a source line you can check.
- **Two-source rule:** a fact ships only if confirmed across **two independent, reputable**
  sources (encyclopedias, museums, university pages, fact-checked obituaries, primary docs).
  Never quote-mills (BrainyQuote, AZQuotes, Pinterest); never agency images sold as free.
- **Demote, don't dress up:** thin sourcing → drop the detail or label it "approximate." If the
  central claim is contested/unratified, **cut the person** and log why.
- Quotes must trace to the actual speech/book/interview, not an aggregator.

## 2. Originality Standard
- Treat source material (incl. public-domain images) as **raw material, not finished content**.
- Every post must add **at least three original contribution layers** from: researched context ·
  original written framing · custom visual composition · source citation / provenance note ·
  original caption · design treatment.
- A light overlay, repost, screenshot, watermark, or minimal crop is **not enough**.
- Do **not** claim "Instagram counts public-domain + overlay as original" — hold our own
  stricter bar instead.

## 3. Voice
- Calm, plain, grounded, respectful, quietly powerful. **No hype, no emojis on the card.**
- Don't flatten real people into slogans. Inspirational, never fake or manipulative.

## 4. The human gate (automate the labor, never the judgment)
- The system researches, drafts, renders, and stages as `pending`. **A human approves before
  anything posts.** Never auto-approve, never invent a person/date/quote/source.

## 5. Card fields
`subject_name` · `hook` · `body` · `copy_today` (shown as DO THIS TODAY) · `receipt` ·
`image_credit` · `caption` · `first_comment` · `palette` · `status`.
