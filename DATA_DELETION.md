# Data Deletion Request — The Hard Win

*Effective date: 2026-07-01. This page may be updated as the system changes.*

This page explains what data The Hard Win may store about comments on our posts, and how to
ask us to delete the data we hold about you.

---

## What data may be stored

For comments left on our Instagram posts, our review system may store:

- Your public Instagram **username**
- The **comment text**
- The **comment ID**
- The **post / media ID** the comment was on
- **Timestamps**
- The **reply draft and status** (for example: needs review, approved, posted)
- Any **moderation / review notes** we added

This is public interaction data from our own posts, kept so we can moderate comments and
review suggested replies before a human approves them.

---

## How to request deletion

To ask us to delete the review data we hold about your comment(s), send a request to:

**Contact:** _[INSERT PUBLIC CONTACT EMAIL FOR THE HARD WIN]_

Please include:

1. Your **Instagram username**.
2. A **link to or description of** the comment/post involved.
3. A clear statement that you want us to **delete the associated comment-reply review data**.

---

## What happens next

- We will **review the request** and delete the associated review data we hold within a
  **reasonable period**.
- We may confirm your request or ask for enough detail to locate the correct records.

---

## Important: Instagram-controlled content

We can only delete data from **our own review system**. We **cannot** delete content that
Instagram controls.

- If you want your **comment removed from Instagram itself**, please **delete it directly in
  the Instagram app**. Once a comment is deleted on Instagram, it is no longer available to our
  system.
- Removing a comment on Instagram does not require contacting us — you control your own comments
  on the platform.

---

## No secrets

This page is public. It contains no tokens, credentials, API keys, or private addresses — and
requests should never include passwords or private account credentials.
