# Privacy Policy — The Hard Win

*Effective date: 2026-07-01. This policy may be updated as the system changes; the effective
date above will change when it does.*

The Hard Win is an inspirational history and culture content system that publishes posts to
Instagram and helps manage the comments on those posts. This policy explains, in plain
language, what public information the system may process, why, and how it is protected.

---

## What data we may process

The Hard Win may process **public Instagram interaction data** connected to our own posts,
such as:

- Instagram **usernames** of people who comment
- **Public comments** (the text people leave on our posts)
- **Comment IDs**
- **Post / media IDs**
- **Timestamps**
- **Reply status** (for example: needs review, approved, posted)
- **Moderation / review notes** we add while reviewing a comment

We only work with information that is already **public** on Instagram (comments on our own
posts). We do not collect private messages, email addresses, phone numbers, location, or
payment information through this system.

---

## Why we use it

We use this data to run the account responsibly:

- **Content management** — publishing and organizing our own Instagram posts
- **Comment moderation** — sorting comments (e.g. praise, questions, spam) and deciding what
  needs a response
- **AI-drafted reply suggestions** — drafting a *suggested* reply for a human to review
- **Human-reviewed public replies** — publishing a reply only after a person approves it
- **Troubleshooting** — diagnosing and fixing automation failures

---

## AI drafts, humans approve

Our system may use automated tools (AI) to **draft** suggested replies to comments. **No
public reply is ever posted automatically.** Every public reply requires a person to review
and approve it first. AI drafts; a human decides.

---

## What we do **not** do

- We **do not sell** personal data.
- We **do not** share this data for advertising or with data brokers.
- We **do not** use it for any purpose beyond running and improving this content account.

---

## How data and credentials are protected

- **Tokens, API keys, and app secrets are private.** They are stored locally on the operator's
  machine or in secure environment settings — **never** in public GitHub files.
- **Logs that contain commenter text are kept private** and are excluded from the code
  repository (gitignored), so they are not published.
- Review data is stored in a secured database with access restricted to the account operator.

---

## Data deletion

If you want data associated with your comment removed from our review system, see
[`DATA_DELETION.md`](DATA_DELETION.md). Note that to remove a comment from **Instagram
itself**, you must delete it in the Instagram app — that content is controlled by Instagram,
not by us.
